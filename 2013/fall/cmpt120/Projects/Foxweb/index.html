Biography

My name’s Ethan, and I’m a computer science major at Marist College in Poughkeepsie, New York. I’m also a web developer at Fanzter, helping them develop new products.

For my senior capstone project in high school, I created Noted, a tool for students to organize their research. Along with Noted, I’m developing Drafts, an easy way for students to take notes. They’re both still in development, and hopefully will be ready for college students to use for the fall semester.

Making web applications really is a passion of mine. At this point, I’m fairly proficient at Ruby and Ruby on Rails (though I prefer Padrino), but in the past I’ve used Node.js, PHP, and Java.
