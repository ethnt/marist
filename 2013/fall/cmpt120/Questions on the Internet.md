_Who started the Internet?_

DARPA, a government agency, started the Internet. It was originally between the University of California Santa Barbara, University of Utah, and University of San Francisco.

***

_What is the main protocol of the Internet?_

TCP/IP is the main protocol of the Internet. HTTP is used for the World Wide Web.

***

_Who invented the World Wide Web?_

Tim Berners-Lee invented the first web server and web browser.

***

_How can we describe the difference between the Internet and the World Wide Web?_

The Internet is the interconnectedness of the computers and devices. The World Wide Web is the HTTP layer on top of TCP/IP.
