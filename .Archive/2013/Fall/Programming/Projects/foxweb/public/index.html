    Biography

    My name's Ethan, and I'm a first-year computer science major at Marist College in Poughkeepsie, New York.

    I've been interested in computers since a very young age. I created my first website with HTML in 6th grade, and from there, I learned CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and eventually Ruby.

    For my senior capstone project in high school, I created Noted, a tool for students to organize their research. Along with Noted, I'm creating Recall, a simple planner for students so they can keep track of their assignments.

    There are various projects of mine on GitHub. Nearly all of my work is open source, for anyone to use.
